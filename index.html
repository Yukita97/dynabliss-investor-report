The Story: Six Years of Building, Now Ready to Monetize

DynaBliss has built a purpose-built practice management platform that 459 acupuncture and chiropractic clinics depend on daily — growing 48.9% year-over-year to $38,073 MRR ($456K ARR), with net revenue of $407,615 over the past 12 months and an improving churn rate now at just 1.08%/month. The platform's customer lifetime value stands at $7,681 — a figure that continues to climb.

The company is at an inflection point. In March 2026 alone, $1.66 million in patient payments flowed through the platform via Stripe — an annualized volume of ~$20M — generating zero revenue for DynaBliss. That changes with the Business WYZE initiative. Following a meeting with FluidPay (a payment gateway processing 3,500+ merchant accounts), the team has a clear path: separate the gateway from the underwriting layer, price competitively below Jane App's 2.6–2.7% rate, and capture the margin. Even a 0.5% net margin on existing volume adds ~$8,300/month — with zero new customer acquisition.

Meanwhile, the company just achieved its strongest financial position ever: $55,000 in credit card debt fully paid off. AI Scribe usage exceeds 11,000 sessions per month. Security investments (pen testing + cyber insurance) are underway. And the team has sustained 156 consecutive weekly sprints of product delivery without interruption.

🔑 The FluidPay Opportunity — Meeting Completed April 2026

The team held a full strategy session with Joe from FluidPay — a payment gateway handling 3,500+ merchant accounts and currently migrating clients off Stripe. The meeting produced a clear, actionable path to capturing revenue from the $1.66M/month already flowing through the platform.

The model: separate the gateway layer (FluidPay) from the underwriting/acquiring layer (via a partner ISO). FluidPay handles the tech — API, hosted payment pages, recurring billing, fraud protection — at $5/month per processor ($10/mo for ACH + credit card) plus $0.05/API call. The underwriter handles KYC/KYB, merchant applications, and interchange fees. DynaBliss sits in between, setting practitioner rates and keeping the margin.

Competitive benchmark: Jane App charges 2.6–2.7%. Square charges 3.5%+. DynaBliss's target: price below Jane (competitive advantage) while maintaining a real margin. With the right underwriter rate (~1.5–2.0% interchange cost), a 2.4–2.5% practitioner rate leaves a 0.5–1.0% net margin on every dollar processed.

Risks & Challenges
Honest assessment — each risk has an active mitigation

🔴 Payment Monetization Not Yet Live

Risk: $1.66M/month flows through Stripe with zero revenue capture. Until Business WYZE launches, this is an opportunity cost, not realized revenue.
Mitigation: FluidPay meeting completed April 2026. Ed (underwriter) outreach this week. ISV partner intro scheduled. The volume already exists — this is a 90-day execution window, not a product build.

🟡 KYB Onboarding Friction for Practitioners

Risk: Moving from Stripe (which absorbs all merchant onboarding) to a new payment stack requires each practice to go through KYC/KYB. A prior competitor cited this as a churn reason.
Mitigation: Team is actively modeling whether to absorb PCI fees or pass through. ISV partner has streamlined onboarding. Getting ahead of this before launch.

🟡 Support Scalability (Pre-AI Chatbot)

Risk: 459+ practices generate increasing support requests. Manual support doesn't scale linearly.
Mitigation: AI Support Chatbot (L1/L2) actively in development. Intercom infrastructure in place. Estimated H1 2026 launch.

🟡 ChiroBliss Traction Below Potential

Risk: ChiroBliss launched January 2024 with strong infrastructure but represents a small share of the active base today.
Mitigation: Website redesign + Handcraft Health partnership expected to accelerate chiro-specific acquisition. The platform is built — distribution is the lever.

🟢 HIPAA / PHI Security

Risk: Platform handles Protected Health Information and payment data — breach would be costly and reputationally damaging.
Mitigation: Penetration testing starting immediately. $8K cyber insurance budget allocated. Proactive, not reactive.

🟢 Competitive Landscape

Risk: Jane App and other general-purpose EHR tools compete in adjacent spaces with greater resources.
Mitigation: Purpose-built platforms for acupuncture/chiro have deeper workflow fit. AI Scribe, insurance billing, and upcoming payment processing at competitive rates create a moat that generalists won't prioritize for niche verticals. Churn rate improvement to 1.08% suggests the moat is working.
